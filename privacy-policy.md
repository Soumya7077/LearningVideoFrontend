Last updated: 2025-12-09

PARICHAY CLASSES (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the PARICHAY CLASSES mobile application (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information
When users create an account or sign in, we may collect:
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number (if provided)
- Password (securely stored using Firebase authentication)

1.2 Usage Data
We may collect information about:
- The content you view (course videos, chapters)
- Features you interact with
- Time spent on the app


2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information for:
- Creating and managing user accounts
- Providing course videos, chapters, and PDF materials
- Improving app performance and user experience
- Maintaining security and preventing unauthorized activity
- Communicating updates or service-related information

3. Firebase Services Used

3.1 Firebase Authentication
Used for login and signup.

3.2 Firebase Firestore
Used to store and manage:
- User profile data
- Course access and progress
- In-app usage and activity

Firebase may automatically collect:
- Device information
- App performance data

4. Data Sharing
We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.
We may share information only with:
- Service Providers (Firebase) for app functionality
- Law enforcement if required by law

5. Data Security
We take the protection of your data seriously and use:
- Firebase secure storage
- Encrypted transmission (HTTPS)
- Restricted access within the database

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Children’s Privacy
Our App is intended for students and general users.
We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent.
